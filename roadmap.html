🚣️ Project Roadmap – Vigilant Angel AI

For the Most Vulnerable People — Spiritually Guided, Technologically Armed

✅ Phase 1 – MVP Release (In Progress)

Mission: Empower individuals and families with faith-aligned, offline-first protection — offering clear guidance in moments of confusion, fear, or crisis.

  • 📆 Core Knowledge Modules (KMs)
    • First Aid & Emergency Care
    • U.S. Constitution & Individual Rights
    • Biblical Wisdom (KJV)
    • Logic and Critical Thinking
    • Inner Strength & Resilience
  • 🧐 Vigilant Angel Setup Assistant
    • Step-by-step setup with helpful prompts
    • Configures filters, alerts, and local features
    • Simple enough for non-technical users to follow
  • 🌐 Network Guardian Suite
    • Pi-hole for DNS-level blocking (ads, scams, explicit content)
    • Suricata for intrusion detection and threat monitoring
    • Internal protections* silently operate in the background
    • Manual block controls for common threat domains
  • 🔐 Privacy-First Architecture
    • Offline by default — no cloud, no telemetry, no tracking
    • Fully local storage, processing, and access
    • Encrypted backup and update capability
  • 🤝 Spiritually Guided Ethos
    • Rooted in stewardship, not surveillance
    • Promotes peace, wisdom, and protection
    • Offers moral clarity and gentle encouragement
  • Note: Capabilities depend on the hardware it's installed on. Higher-end devices enable more advanced features such as image recognition or multi-user support.

🔄 Phase 2 – Community & Expansion

  • ✉️ Message Protection & Email Scam Detection
    • Flags risky or deceptive content
    • Translates technical threats into clear language
    • Optional caregiver alerts via SMS
    • Internals obfuscated to reduce the chance of evasion
  • 📲 Mobile Companion App
    • LAN-connected Home Mode
    • Offline Emergency Mode with cached content
    • Remote access via VPN
    • Optional SMS/Bluetooth-based alerts
  • 🛠️ Specialty Knowledge Modules
    • Parenting, homesteading, spiritual support, preparedness
    • Variants tailored for pastors, teachers, veterans, and more
  • 📆 Community-Curated Wisdom Packs
    • Downloadable knowledge bundles
    • Created and maintained by trusted users
    • Faith-aligned and resilience-focused
  • 🌱 Invitation to Build Together
    • Vigilant Angel AI is more than software — it’s a mission
    • Anyone of any skill level can help — from idea sharing and testing to editing and writing
    • Every contribution strengthens protection for all
    • If you feel called to serve, there's a place for you here

🧪 Phase 3 – Resilience & Networked Deployment

  • 🚁 Grid-Down Emergency Mode
    • Continues operating during prolonged power or internet outages
    • Ensures critical knowledge access and system functionality
  • 🔍 Offline Visual Recognition
    • Identifies plants, mushrooms, medications, and pests
    • Useful for safety, gardening, or homesteading support
  • 🛠️ Self-Healing System Checks
    • Routine integrity and health checks
    • Auto-repair with local rollback and recovery
  • 📷 Optional Local Camera Integration
    • Image processing occurs entirely offline
    • Never stored, uploaded, or shared
  • 📡 Neighborhood Kits & Multi-Node Support
    • Supports LAN-based deployments for clinics, churches, and off-grid communities
    • Mesh-ready design allows sharing of updates, alerts, and wisdom packs locally
  • 📊 Guardian Dashboard
    • Shows system health, blocked threats, and summary alerts
    • Offers guidance and spiritual encouragement to the user

*Some internal security mechanisms are intentionally obfuscated to maintain operational integrity and avoid exposure to circumvention or tampering. Vigilant Angel AI guards quietly and faithfully.
