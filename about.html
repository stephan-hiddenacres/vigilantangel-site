🌐 Why Offline Capability Matters

Cloud AI fails when:

  • ⛈️ A natural disaster, cyberattack, or political disruption severs internet access
  • 🕵️‍♂️ Sensitive queries shouldn't leave your home
  • 🌳 You live or travel off-grid (rural areas, remote work, intentional disconnection)

Vigilant Angel keeps going:

  • 💻 Runs on local hardware: Raspberry Pi, NUC, laptop, or dedicated box

  • 📶 Accessible from any LAN device — even your phone
